Tickets and donations

EHSM is entirely supported by its attendees and donors. To help us make this event happen, please donate and/or order your ticket as soon as possible.

1. Make your payment

Wire transfer

  • 55€ - early registration fee (ends August 5th!).
  • 75€ - regular registration fee (ends October 1st!).
  • 90€ - late registration fee (after October 1st).
  • 230€ - supporter ticket, with our thanks and your name on the website.
  • 1100€ - gold supporter ticket, with our thanks and your company/project logo on the website and the printed programme.

Proceed to the wire transfer payment system

Google Checkout

Even though the prices are in USD (due to the lack of a halfway decent online payment system that accepts EUR directly), European payment cards are accepted.

  • $65 - early registration fee (ends August 5th!).
  • $90 - regular registration fee (ends October 1st!).
  • $110 - late registration fee (after October 1st).
  • $280 - supporter ticket, with our thanks and your name on the website.
  • $1337 - gold supporter ticket, with our thanks and your company/project logo on the website and the printed programme.

Minors (-18) can get into EHSM for free accompanied by a responsible adult (who needs a ticket).

2. Check your email for the ticket

We will generate electronic tickets and send them to the email address you provided during payment. Be patient - we may not get to it before months - but if you have received the payment confirmation you can be sure that you will have your ticket in time.

3. Print your ticket

Print your ticket and present it on the days of the conference.

Just want to donate?

Select "€ 0.00 - DONATION ONLY" in our ticket system, and fill in a donation amount (no minimum). Thanks for your support!
