Tickets and donations

The ticket pre-sale is over since Dec 16th. We will email your tickets on December 20th. If you are paying by wire transfer, please try to do the transfer soon enough so that we receive the money by December 20.

If you have not received your ticket after Dec 21st, contact msach at mailbox dot tu-berlin dot de.

The following tickets will be available at the door:

  • 100€ - 3 day pass
  • 50€ - 3 day pass for students (proof required)
  • 35€ - day ticket
Please bring the exact amount.

Minors (-18) can get into EHSM for free accompanied by a responsible adult (who needs a ticket).
