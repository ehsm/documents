Tickets and donations

EHSM is entirely supported by its attendees and donors. To help us make this event happen, please donate and/or order your ticket as soon as possible.

Electronic ticket

1. Choose an amount and make your payment

Donations of these minimal amounts grant you various goodies and/or access to the conference. Payments are processed with Google Checkout.

Even though the prices are in USD (due to the lack of a good online payment system that accepts EUR directly), European payment cards are accepted.

  • $45 - day ticket, valid for any single day of the conference.
  • $65 - early registration fee (ends July 15th!). Ticket valid for all 3 days.
  • $110 - regular registration fee (after July 15th). Ticket valid for all 3 days.
  • $280 - supporter ticket, with our thanks and your name on the website.
  • $1337 - gold supporter ticket, with our thanks and your company/project logo on the website and the printed programme.

2. Check your email for the ticket

We will generate electronic tickets and send them to the email address you provided during payment. Be patient - we may not get to it before months - but if you have received the payment confirmation you can be sure that you will have your ticket in time.

3. Print your ticket

Print your ticket and present it on the days of the conference.

Cash sales

We will be opening cash sales shortly in Berlin. This will allow you to pay in Euro and remain anonymous. Stay tuned.
