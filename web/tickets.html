Tickets and donations

EHSM is entirely supported by its attendees and sponsors. To help us make this event happen, please donate and/or order your ticket as soon as possible.

Prices

Donations of these minimal amounts grant you various goodies and/or access to the conference. Payments are processed with Google Checkout. We are flexible, so feel free to contact us at orga AT ehsm.eu if you have special requirements or ideas.

  • $45 - day ticket, valid for any single day of the conference.
  • $65 - early registration fee (ends July 15th!). Ticket valid for all 3 days.
  • $110 - regular registration fee (after July 15th). Ticket valid for all 3 days.
  • $280 - supporter ticket, with our thanks and your name on the website.
  • $1337 - gold supporter ticket, with our thanks and your company/project logo on the website and the printed programme.
