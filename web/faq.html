Why is it at the same time as the 29C3?

  • The EHSM theme is complementary to that of the CCC, the latter having a strong focus on security and politics and little focus on open hardware. We want to contribute on the latter.
  • You can easily go to both events, and transportation between the BCC and TU is < 30min. CCC is 4 days, EHSM is 3.
  • The CCC has become so popular that it has had to refuse people for the last few years.
  • There are actually hackers who travel to Berlin at those dates just to hang around. Seize this opportunity to learn about sides of hardware hacking you might have never seen.
  • It's easier to get a room during this period. We can save money on the room and spend more on the content.

I want to speak. When should I submit?

  • As soon as possible and before October 15th.
  • We will confirm and announce some speakers approximately every month (depending on ticket sales).
  • Submitting early gives you more time to prepare your travel.

Can I go there if I cannot afford the ticket price?

  • Yes. Write a note to orga AT ehsm dot eu to propose your help. In exchange for a few hours of volunteering, you can enjoy the rest of the conference. All volunteering agreements have to be confirmed at least one month before the start of the conference.
  • Yes. Crash the gate and don't get caught.
