Do I need a ticket for my child?

Minors (-18) can get in for free but must be accompanied by a responsible adult who needs a ticket.

Why is it at the same time as a hacker conference?

The 600000 square meters of the TU Berlin campus are pretty empty at those dates and it becomes possible to have free high-quality conference space then. This allows us to spend money where it matters: on the content.
